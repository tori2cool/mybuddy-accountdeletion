Delete MyBuddy Account

To delete your MyBuddy account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the MyBuddy app on your device.
  2. Go to your profile.
  3. Tap "Delete My Parent Account".
  4. Confirm with your parent PIN.

This action is permanent. It will delete the parent account and all associated child profiles and data.

If you have any questions, please contact us at info@mybuddy-ai.com
